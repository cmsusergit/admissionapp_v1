# Fee Collector User Guide

This guide is for staff responsible for collecting admission fees and issuing receipts.

## 1. Dashboard Overview

After logging in as a **Fee Collector**, you will see:
*   **Today's Collection:** Total amount collected today.
*   **Pending Payments:** Number of students who have not yet paid.
*   **Cleared Payments:** Number of students with successful transactions.

---

## 2. Fee Collection

### Searching for a Student
1.  Go to the **Fee Collection** tab.
2.  Enter the **Application ID**, **Student Name**, or **Mobile Number** in the search bar.
3.  Click **Search**.
4.  Select the student from the results.

### Verifying Payment Details
1.  Check the **Fee Structure** displayed.
    *   Tuition Fee, Exam Fee, etc.
    *   Total Amount Due.
2.  **Payment Mode:** Select the method used by the student (Cash, Cheque, DD, Online Transfer).
3.  **Transaction Details:** Enter the Cheque/DD Number, Bank Name, Date.

### Processing Payment
1.  Click **Collect Fee**.
2.  Confirm the amount received.
3.  The system will generate a **Receipt Number**.
4.  **Print Receipt:** Click to print immediately or download as PDF.

---

## 3. Reconciliation

### Daily Closing
1.  Go to the **Reports** section.
2.  Select **Daily Collection Report**.
3.  Check the total cash/cheque collected against physical cash.
4.  **Submit Closing:** Enter the closing balance and submit for verification.

### Search Transactions
1.  Go to **Transaction History**.
2.  Filter by Date, Payment Mode, or Student.
3.  View details of past transactions.
4.  **Reprint Receipt:** If a student requests a duplicate receipt.

---

## 4. Troubleshooting

*   **Payment Mismatch:** If the system shows "Pending" but the student claims to have paid online.
    *   Check the **Transaction ID** provided by the student.
    *   Verify with the bank portal.
    *   Update status manually if confirmed.
*   **Refunds:** For cancelled admissions.
    *   Go to the student's payment record.
    *   Click **Initiate Refund**.
    *   Enter reason and amount.
    *   Submit for approval.
