# Student User Guide

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for students using the University Admission System.

## 1. Account Creation and Login

### Registration
1.  Navigate to the **Home Page**.
2.  Click on the **Register** button in the top navigation bar.
3.  Fill in the registration form with your:
    *   Full Name
    *   Valid Email Address (this will be your username)
    *   Mobile Number
    *   Password
4.  Click **Sign Up**.
5.  Check your email inbox for a verification link. Click the link to activate your account.

### Login
1.  Click the **Login** button.
2.  Enter your registered Email and Password.
3.  Click **Sign In**.
4.  You will be redirected to your **Student Dashboard**.

---

## 2. Filling an Application Form

### Starting an Application
1.  On your **Dashboard**, locate the **Open Admission Cycles** section.
2.  Find the course/college you wish to apply for.
3.  Click the **Apply Now** button.

### completing the Form
The application form is divided into sections. You must complete them in order.

**Step 1: Personal Details**
1.  Enter your personal information (Date of Birth, Gender, Category, Address, etc.).
2.  Ensure all fields marked with a red asterisk (*) are filled.
3.  Click **Save & Next**.

**Step 2: Academic History**
1.  Enter details of your previous qualifications (e.g., 10th, 12th, Bachelor's).
2.  Enter the marks obtained, total marks, and percentage/CGPA.
3.  Click **Save & Next**.

**Step 3: Document Upload**
1.  You will see a list of required documents (e.g., Photo, Signature, Marksheets).
2.  Click **Choose File** for each document.
3.  Select the file from your device (Allowed formats: JPG, PNG, PDF; Max size: 2MB).
4.  Click **Upload** for each file. A preview or success message will appear.
5.  Click **Save & Next** once all required documents are uploaded.

**Step 4: Review and Submit**
1.  Review all the information you have entered.
2.  Check the **Declaration** box confirming the details are true.
3.  Click **Submit Application**.
    *   *Warning:* Once submitted, you cannot edit the form unless requested by the college.

---

## 3. Post-Submission

### Tracking Status
1.  Go to your **Dashboard**.
2.  Look at the **My Applications** section.
3.  The status will be one of the following:
    *   **Submitted:** Waiting for college verification.
    *   **Under Review:** College is currently checking your details.
    *   **Needs Correction:** See below.
    *   **Approved:** Your application is verified.
    *   **Rejected:** Your application was not accepted.

### Handling Corrections
If your status is **Needs Correction**:
1.  Click on the application to view details.
2.  Look for the **Remarks** or **Rejection Reason** section (e.g., "12th Marksheet is blurry").
3.  Go to the **Document Upload** section.
4.  Re-upload the specific document requested.
5.  Click **Resubmit**.

---

## 4. Merit Lists and Admission

1.  On the announced date, log in to check the **Merit List**.
2.  If your name appears in the list, you will see an **Admission Offer**.
3.  Click **Accept Offer** to proceed.

---

## 5. Fee Payment

1.  After accepting the offer, navigate to the **Payments** section.
2.  Review the fee breakdown.
3.  Click **Pay Now** to be redirected to the payment gateway.
4.  Complete the transaction using Credit Card, Debit Card, or Net Banking.
5.  Once successful, you can download your **Fee Receipt** and **Admission Letter**.
